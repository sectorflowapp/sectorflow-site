01

Who this policy covers

This Privacy Policy applies to the current publicly available version of SectorFlow and to the SectorFlow website. "SectorFlow", "we", "us", or "our" refers to the developer of this application.

SectorFlow is an iOS pilot utility and planning support app. The launch app supports iOS 18 and later. Current launch scope is focused on pilot utility tools such as time calculations, clock calculations, time zone calculations, crew rest planning, and sunrise/sunset support.

Some features described on our website or roadmap may not yet be available in the app. Future features, such as advanced briefing or document-analysis modules, will be covered by updated privacy terms if they change how data is handled.

Operational note: SectorFlow is not an operational authority and is not a replacement for official sources, SOPs, company manuals, OFPs, NOTAMs, charts, regulations, or pilot judgment.

02

Current app data handling

✦

The current app does not require an account, sell personal information, or use third-party advertising tracking. Support submissions are different: if you choose to contact us via the website contact form or the in-app feedback function, we process the information you provide so we can respond.

For normal use of the current app, calculation inputs and utility data are processed on device unless you voluntarily send information to SectorFlow for support. This means we do not receive the values you enter into pilot utility tools during ordinary app use.

The current app does not require:

  • An account, profile, or sign-in
  • Advertising identifiers or third-party advertising tracking
  • Sale of personal information
  • Upload of time calculator, clock, time zone, crew rest, or sunrise/sunset inputs for normal calculations

This is not a global statement that no data is ever processed. Information may be processed when you voluntarily contact support, when website infrastructure serves or protects the site, or when Apple provides developers with App Store Connect information under Apple's own systems and user/device settings.

03

On-device processing

SectorFlow is built to keep current utility calculations local where practical. Examples of app data processed on your device include:

  • Time calculator inputs
  • Clock and time zone calculations
  • Crew rest planning inputs
  • Sunrise and sunset inputs, including manually entered coordinates or device location if you choose to use location-based calculation

These utility inputs are processed on your device and are not sent to SectorFlow servers unless you voluntarily share them with us, for example in a support message.

If an app feature lets you export, copy, paste, share, or import data through iOS system features, that action is under your control. Please avoid sharing operationally sensitive flight details, passenger information, company-confidential information, or other unnecessary sensitive data with SectorFlow support.

04

Location-based features

If you choose to use location-based features, SectorFlow may request access to your device location through iOS. Location is used to calculate relevant in-app results, such as sunrise and sunset estimates where available.

For the current launch app, location-based calculations are designed to be processed on your device. SectorFlow does not use location for advertising or tracking. SectorFlow does not receive your precise location during ordinary use unless you voluntarily include it in a support or feedback message.

Location access can be disabled in iOS Settings. If you disable location access, location-based results may be unavailable or may require manual input.

05

Support and contact information

If you contact SectorFlow via the website contact form or the in-app feedback function, we may receive your email address, message content, and any information you choose to include. The website support form may also ask for details such as your name and subject so we can handle the request. In-app feedback may include user-submitted feedback and app/device context necessary to understand the issue.

If an in-app feedback submission cannot be sent because your device is offline, the request times out, or the service is temporarily unavailable, the app may temporarily save the pending feedback on your device and retry when connectivity returns. Pending feedback is intended to be deleted from local storage after successful submission.

We use support information only to respond to your request, support you, improve reliability, protect the service from abuse, or maintain necessary records.

Support messages are voluntary. Do not include operationally sensitive flight details, passenger information, company-confidential information, or other unnecessary sensitive data unless it is needed for your request.

06

Website analytics and infrastructure

The SectorFlow website is separate from the iOS app. The website may use hosting, security, anti-spam, email delivery, and privacy-preserving analytics or infrastructure providers to serve pages, protect forms, deliver support messages, monitor reliability, and understand basic website performance.

This does not mean the iOS app itself collects analytics. The website may use Cloudflare services, Cloudflare Turnstile, GitHub Pages or GitHub hosting, and a contact endpoint at contact.sectorflowapp.com.

Website and infrastructure providers may process technical information such as IP address, browser or device information, request metadata, spam-prevention signals, and similar logs needed to provide their services. SectorFlow does not use this information to sell personal data or build advertising profiles.

07

Third-party services

SectorFlow may rely on third-party services that are visible in the website repository or support flow, including:

  • GitHub or GitHub Pages for website hosting and repository infrastructure
  • Cloudflare for website security, performance, anti-spam, and privacy-preserving analytics or infrastructure
  • Cloudflare Turnstile for spam and abuse prevention on the website support form
  • Resend or the configured email delivery provider for support message delivery, if used by the deployed support Worker
  • Apple services such as the App Store, TestFlight, or App Store Connect, governed by Apple's own privacy practices

SectorFlow does not use third-party app analytics, advertising SDKs, or social login providers unless a future app version or website update clearly discloses that change.

08

Data retention

App utility data remains on your device unless you voluntarily share it. Locally stored app data remains on your device until you delete it through the app, clear related local storage if available, or delete the app.

Pending in-app feedback saved for retry remains on your device until it is successfully submitted, deleted by app logic, or removed when you delete the app. After successful submission, the pending local copy is intended to be deleted.

Support messages received by SectorFlow may be retained as long as needed to respond, maintain records, comply with obligations, improve support and reliability, and prevent abuse.

09

Children's privacy

SectorFlow is not directed to children under 13. SectorFlow does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10

International processing

If you contact SectorFlow, your information may be processed where SectorFlow or its service providers operate. This may include locations outside your country or region.

11

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data under laws such as the GDPR (European Union), PDPA (Singapore), or CCPA (California). These include rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data.

Because SectorFlow does not require app accounts and current utility data is generally processed on device, many requests will have no app-side account records for us to provide, correct, or delete. If you previously submitted feedback or a support request, use the website contact form or the in-app feedback function for privacy-related questions.

12

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the Last updated date at the top of this page and notify users via an in-app notice or App Store release notes where appropriate.

✦

Future features: Some roadmap features may have different privacy terms when released. If a future released feature changes how data is handled, we will update this policy and explain the change clearly.
