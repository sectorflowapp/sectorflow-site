01

Who this policy covers

This Privacy Policy applies to the About Flying iOS app and this website. “About Flying”, “we”, “us”, or “our” refers to the developer of the app and website.

About Flying is an iOS pilot utility and planning-support app. The reviewed release includes Home, Time Tools, Crew Rest, Sunrise & Sunset, Settings, feedback, and user-approved crash reporting.

Operational note: About Flying is not an operational authority and is not a replacement for official sources, SOPs, company manuals, OFPs, NOTAMs, charts, regulations, or pilot judgment.

02

Current app data handling

✦

The app does not require an account, sell personal information, or use third-party advertising tracking. Feedback, crash reports, and website support submissions are separate user-initiated flows described below.

For normal app use, calculation inputs are processed on device. We do not receive the values entered into pilot tools unless a user chooses to include them in a feedback or support message.

The app does not add these identifiers to feedback or crash submissions:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Vendor identifiers
  • Installation identifiers
  • Account identifiers
  • Persistent device identifiers

Generic device class values such as “iPhone” and broad processor architecture values such as arm64 describe the environment; they are not persistent device identifiers.

03

On-device processing

About Flying processes the following pilot-tool data on your device:

  • Time calculator inputs
  • Clock and time zone calculations
  • Crew rest planning inputs
  • Sunrise and sunset inputs, including manually entered coordinates or device location if you choose to use location-based calculation

These utility inputs are not sent to the feedback endpoint during normal calculations. A user may voluntarily type information into a feedback or website support message.

Export, copy, paste, QR sharing, and import actions are under the user's control. Avoid sharing unnecessary operationally sensitive flight details, passenger information, or company-confidential information with support.

04

Location-based features

If you choose GPS mode, About Flying requests location access through iOS and uses the location on device to calculate Sunrise & Sunset results.

About Flying does not use location for advertising or tracking and does not automatically add precise location to a feedback or crash report. We may receive location only if a user voluntarily includes it in a message.

Location access can be disabled in iOS Settings. If you disable location access, location-based results may be unavailable or may require manual input.

05

Feedback and crash reports

Ordinary in-app feedback includes the selected category, user-authored message, optional reply email, app name, app version, build number, and original creation timestamp. If the user enables “Include basic app/device info”, it also includes the iOS version and a generic device model such as iPhone or iPad.

A crash report is sent only after the user chooses to send it. It has no contact email and may include app version and build, iOS version, generic device class, broad processor architecture, a report-scoped UUID, crash classification and evidence, timestamps, lifecycle context, controlled diagnostic breadcrumbs, bounded memory data, and bounded native crash details such as binary UUIDs and relative stack offsets.

The local queue UUID is not transmitted as an identifier. Reports do not automatically include a route, waypoint, airport, coordinate, altitude input, account identifier, or precise location.

Message content is user controlled. Do not include operationally sensitive flight details, passenger information, company-confidential information, or other unnecessary sensitive data.

06

Local unsent queue

If an in-app submission fails, About Flying stores it in the app's Application Support area and may retry when connectivity returns. Age is measured from the original creation time; retries do not reset it.

A queued report expires at exactly 90 days. The app removes expired reports when it loads the queue and checks expiry again before transmission. Successful submission and the Pending Feedback reset remove local queue records immediately.

The stored field names and schema remain compatible with existing queue data. Date values are absolute instants, so changing the device time zone does not shift the 90-day boundary.

07

Website and infrastructure

The About Flying website is separate from the iOS calculation flows. The reviewed site uses GitHub Pages or GitHub hosting, Cloudflare services and Turnstile, and the contact endpoint at contact.sectorflowapp.com.

The website support form sends the name, email, subject, and message entered by the visitor together with an anti-abuse token. The live destination mailbox, all forwarding processors, provider log contents, and provider-specific retention settings are not verifiable from the public website repository.

Infrastructure providers may process technical information such as IP address, browser or device information, request metadata, spam-prevention signals, and security logs needed to provide their services. About Flying does not deliberately add the device IP address to an app report or use this information to build advertising profiles.

Services visible in the reviewed site include:

  • GitHub or GitHub Pages for website hosting and repository infrastructure
  • Cloudflare for website security, performance, anti-spam, and privacy-preserving analytics or infrastructure
  • Cloudflare Turnstile for spam and abuse prevention on the website support form
  • The configured live delivery provider, which requires administrator-side verification
  • Apple services governed by Apple's own privacy practices

The reviewed app does not contain third-party advertising SDKs, social login, or an app analytics SDK.

08

Data retention

About Flying retains submitted feedback and crash reports under its control for no longer than 90 days, after which they are deleted. Reports may be removed earlier when no longer required. Infrastructure providers may process limited technical or security records according to their own documented retention practices.

Other app settings and pilot-tool inputs remain on the device until changed, reset, or removed with the app, subject to the controls described in the app.

09

Children's privacy

About Flying is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10

International processing

If you contact About Flying, your information may be processed where we or our service providers operate, including outside your country or region.

11

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data under laws such as the GDPR (European Union), PDPA (Singapore), or CCPA (California). These include rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data.

Because About Flying has no app account, public deletion token, or persistent report-to-user index, many requests will have no app-side account record. It may be impossible to identify a particular anonymous report for early deletion. Contact us for privacy questions; the 90-day maximum still applies.

12

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app, website, support process, providers, or legal requirements change. Material changes will update the date at the top of this page.
