01

Who we are

SectorFlow is an offline-first iPhone utility app designed for airline pilots. The launch app provides Time Calculator, Clock Calculator, Time Zone Calculator, Crew Rest planning, and In-Flight Prayer Times.

This Privacy Policy applies to the SectorFlow app for iPhone and the SectorFlow website. "SectorFlow", "we", "us", or "our" refers to the developer of this application.

02

What data we collect

✦

The launch app does not require an account, contain ads, use tracking, or use third-party advertising identifiers. Support submissions are different: if you choose to contact us through the app or website, we process the details you provide so we can respond.

For normal launch-app use, SectorFlow does not collect or upload:

  • No account profile or sign-in details
  • No advertising identifiers or tracking identifiers
  • No usage analytics or feature interaction tracking from an embedded analytics SDK
  • No OFPs, rosters, employee numbers, briefing documents, or operational flight data
  • No flight documents, passenger information, company-confidential information, or crew lists
  • No health or biometric information

Time Calculator data remains on your device. Crew Rest data remains on your device except when you deliberately share or export a plan using system share, QR, copy, paste, or import features. In-Flight Prayer Times data remains on your device during normal use.

If you submit the in-app feedback form, you may provide a message, optional reply email address, feedback type, and, if enabled, basic app/device information such as app version, build number, iOS version, and device model. If SectorFlow detects that the previous app session may not have ended cleanly, it may ask whether you want to send a crash-context report. That report is sent only if you choose to send it and may include app/device information plus the last recorded module/action context.

If you submit the website support form, you may provide your name, reply email address, subject, and message. Website support delivery may also involve technical metadata necessary to deliver, secure, and protect the request. Please do not include operationally sensitive flight details, passenger information, company-confidential information, or other unnecessary sensitive data in support messages.

03

On-device processing

SectorFlow is built on an offline-first architecture. This is a deliberate design decision, not a limitation.

Launch calculations in SectorFlow — duration arithmetic, 24-hour clock arithmetic, time zone conversion, UTC rest planning, countdowns, and prayer-time estimation — are processed on your iPhone. They are not uploaded for calculation.

Core launch tools are designed to work without an internet connection. App feedback and crash-context reports require network access because they are deliberately submitted to support.

Prayer Times may use location only when you explicitly request GPS-based calculation. Location is used for the calculation and should not be understood as continuous tracking. Route, waypoint, and manual inputs are processed on device.

04

Sharing, tracking, and AI training

SectorFlow does not sell user data. The launch app does not contain ads, does not use tracking, does not use third-party advertising identifiers, and does not train AI models on your data.

The launch app does not upload OFPs, rosters, employee numbers, briefing documents, or operational flight data. It does not include cloud AI processing in the launch scope.

The app does not integrate any of the following:

  • Advertising networks or ad SDKs
  • Analytics platforms (e.g. Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude)
  • Crash reporting services (e.g. Crashlytics, Sentry)
  • Customer data platforms or CRMs
  • Social media SDKs or login providers
  • Cloud storage or sync services

Server-side support handling, including Resend or the configured email delivery provider, may process support form submissions to deliver email. The support destination is intentionally not exposed in public client-side JavaScript.

The app also interacts with Apple's App Store for download and updates, which is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

05

Cloudflare website services

We may use Cloudflare and GitHub hosting services to operate and secure the SectorFlow website. These providers may process standard web server logs and technical metadata needed to serve, protect, monitor, and troubleshoot the website.

Cloudflare Turnstile may be used on the website support form to reduce spam and abuse.

Cloudflare may process limited technical information such as IP address, browser or device information, request metadata, and interaction signals needed to provide these services. SectorFlow does not use Cloudflare to sell personal data or build advertising profiles.

06

Analytics and diagnostics

The SectorFlow iPhone app is designed without an embedded analytics SDK at launch. We do not use the app to track which modules you open, how frequently you use them, or the values you enter into pilot tools.

Apple may provide developers with App Store Connect analytics, crash logs, diagnostic information, or similar information according to Apple's own systems and user/device settings. This does not mean SectorFlow embeds its own analytics SDK.

The SectorFlow website is different from the app. The website may use Cloudflare services for privacy-preserving analytics, security, spam prevention, and performance. These services help us understand basic website usage and protect forms from abuse without using the information to build advertising profiles.

07

Children's privacy

SectorFlow is designed for professional airline pilots and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children through the app or website support process.

08

Data retention

Since SectorFlow does not maintain app accounts or embedded app analytics profiles, there is no app usage account dataset for us to retain or delete on our end.

Data you store locally — such as saved rest configurations or persisted prayer time fields — is retained on your device until you delete the app or clear its local storage via iPhone Settings. Deleting the app removes all locally stored SectorFlow data permanently.

Support form messages and in-app feedback may be retained only as long as needed to respond, troubleshoot, keep support records, and meet legal or abuse-prevention needs.

09

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data under laws such as the GDPR (European Union), PDPA (Singapore), or CCPA (California). These include rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data.

Because SectorFlow does not maintain app accounts or embedded app analytics profiles, many requests will have no app-side records to provide, correct, or delete. If you previously submitted feedback or a support request, contact us through the in-app feedback form or website contact form for privacy-related questions.

10

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the Last updated date at the top of this page and notify users via an in-app notice or App Store release notes where appropriate.

✦

Future features: Future features may have different privacy terms when released. If a future released feature changes how data is handled, we will update this policy and explain the change clearly.

11

Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or SectorFlow's approach to data, contact the team using the feedback form in the app or the contact form on the website. We aim to respond within 48 hours.

  • App feedback: Open Settings, then Send Feedback.
  • Website support: Use the contact form on the website.
