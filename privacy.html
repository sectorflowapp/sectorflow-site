SectorFlow Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SectorFlow handles information for the SectorFlow iOS app and this support website. It is written to reflect the currently released app functionality: Time Calculator, Clock Calculator, Time Zone Calculator, Crew Rest Planner, and In-Flight Prayer Times.

Introduction

SectorFlow is designed as a privacy-focused pilot utility. The current app does not require account creation, advertising identifiers, third-party marketing trackers, user profiling, sale of personal information, or cross-app tracking.

Information You Voluntarily Provide

If you contact support through the website support form, you may provide your name, email address, and message content. Please do not submit operationally sensitive flight details, passenger information, company-confidential information, or other unnecessary sensitive data.

Contact Form Submissions

Support form submissions are used to respond to support requests, troubleshoot issues, and improve SectorFlow. The current static website prepares support requests through the user's email app. If a server-side support endpoint is introduced later, this policy should be updated to describe that processing.

Device Permissions Actually Used

The current modules may use device capabilities only where needed for their utility function. In-Flight Prayer Times may require location-related input or permission if enabled in the app to estimate prayer time context. SectorFlow does not use location for advertising, profiling, sale of personal information, or cross-app tracking.

Local Storage and Preferences

SectorFlow may store app preferences, recent settings, saved calculation context, or similar utility data locally on your device to support app functionality. Current released modules are intended to work without requiring a user account.

App Privacy Summary

  • No account creation is required for current released functionality.
  • No advertising is included in the current app.
  • No third-party marketing trackers are used by the current app.
  • No user profiling or sale of personal information is part of current functionality.
  • No cross-app tracking is part of current functionality.

Data Security

SectorFlow uses reasonable measures to protect information handled through its app and support process. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, so users should avoid sending unnecessary sensitive information through support messages.

Children's Privacy

SectorFlow is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Future Features

SectorFlow may introduce Flight Briefing AI, OFP Analysis, Route Analysis, and NOTAM Intelligence in future versions. These features are not currently available. If future features change data handling practices, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those features become available.

Changes to This Policy

SectorFlow may update this Privacy Policy as the app, support process, or legal requirements change. The updated date will be revised when material changes are made.

Contact Support Process

Contact support using the support form on the SectorFlow website. The support email address is intentionally not displayed publicly to reduce spam and abuse.
